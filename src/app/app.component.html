notifiy-angular

Simple, yet flexible notifications for Angular2. Why yet another notification library? I found them either too complex or too simplistic and strive for a good balance here. Additionally, it fits in well with Angular Material Design, but is still independent from the material design library!

Simply choose a message to be displayed. The timer (aka delay) determines how long the message will stay on screen.

Most items are pure css properties. This means you can change the color for example by writing red or #FF0000. Similarly you can change the position by 20px or 5em.

Material-design box-shadows are the default, but you can easily turn them off.

The position element is determined by the position attributes. Again, it's just css on a position: fixed notification element.
